## Choosing the Right Model

Each AI model has its strengths:

- **GPT-5**: Excellent for general-purpose tasks, creative writing, and complex reasoning
- **Claude**: Known for careful, nuanced responses and strong analytical capabilities
- **Gemini**: Great at multimodal tasks combining text, images, and data
- **Grok**: Strong in real-time information retrieval and conversational tasks

## Tips for Better Prompts

1. **Be specific**: Instead of "Write about dogs," try "Write a 200-word blog post about the health benefits of adopting rescue dogs."
2. **Provide context**: Tell the AI who you are and what you need the output for.
3. **Iterate**: Don't settle for the first response. Refine your prompts based on what you get.
4. **Use examples**: Show the AI the format or style you want.
